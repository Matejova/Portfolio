Dormbuddy

Type: Project

Client: Fictional

Link: See here (Switch for mobile view)

Most of us had the experience of living in a dorm and it can get quite overwhelming. And that's where Dorm buddy would come in.
As a concept Dorm buddy is your pocket guide on everything that's happening in the dorm - events, news, sell & buy or chatbot style helper that help you with any other problems such as how to operate washing machines or how to get into contact with janitors.

My initial concept

With this project two members wanted to come up with a concept, which would be used. One of those two was me.
This project is for a dorm, which consists of predominantly young adults. And going with the core idea of the app which was to be a buddy, a companion for the resident, I decided to go in the direction of playfulness and friednliness that would be inviting on first look. I created a color palette with tones of the main colors. The idea was that each section would have a color attached to it, except the home page, which would be mixed. It was so that the user that would be coming back already had an association with the color and the content; therefore they could simply navigate the home page. And get faster to the content they were interested in.
As well during this time I got inspired and created a character that would fit this narative. This character was meant to be presented as a part of the onboarding and logo.

This concept however wasnt used due to the majority of project group favored the other, which became the final one.
